# Income Model and Milestones

## Baseline (March 2026)

- Current annual salary equivalent (Silk): ~$36,000 USD ($3K/mo x 12) = ~$58,000 NZD
- Current annual side income: ~$372 USD ($31 MRR VQC x 12) = ~$600 NZD
- Rhythmethod: ~$400-600 NZD/mo (variable, 1-2 shifts/week) = ~$6,000 NZD/yr
- **Current annual total (T0): ~$64,600 NZD (~$40,000 USD)**

## 10x target

- 36-month target annual capacity: `T36 = $640,000 NZD (~$400K USD)`
- Reality check: this means senior/staff design engineer at a US startup ($150-200K) + meaningful product revenue ($50-100K). Ambitious but not insane if NYC move happens.

## Milestone bands

- 12 months (Mar 2027): `2x-3x T0` = $130K-$195K NZD
  - Path: Silk rate increase + first freelance clients + VQC at $200+ MRR + merch revenue
- 24 months (Mar 2028): `4x-6x T0` = $260K-$390K NZD
  - Path: US-based role ($120-180K USD) + side revenue ($20-40K USD)
- 36 months (Mar 2029): `8x-10x T0` = $520K-$640K NZD
  - Path: Senior role ($180-250K USD) + product revenue ($50-100K USD) + music income

- Month 9 (Dec 2026): side income is reliable (non-zero for 3 straight months) ← freelance or app revenue
- Month 18 (Sep 2027): side income reaches 30%-50% of salary baseline ← $1.5-2.5K/mo from products/freelance
- Month 36 (Mar 2029): optionality point (can stay employed by choice, not necessity)
