# Income Model and Milestones

Use this to convert ambition into numeric targets.

## Baseline

- Current annual salary: `S0`
- Current annual side income: `X0`
- Current annual total: `T0 = S0 + X0`

## 10x target

- 36-month target annual capacity: `T36 = 10 * T0`

## Milestone bands

- 12 months target: `2x-3x T0`
- 24 months target: `4x-6x T0`
- 36 months target: `8x-10x T0`
