## Fill this out (v1)

- High-value problem: They have a product vision but can't bridge design → engineering (interactions feel flat, shipping takes too long, no one owns the craft layer)

- Measurable outcome: Production-ready mobile/web app with polished interactions, shipped to App Store / production
- Time window: 4-8 weeks per engagement

- Budget capacity: `medium` ($3K-8K/mo contract or $15K-40K project)
- Urgency level: `high` (shipping deadlines, fundraising demos, launch windows)
- Access to data/systems: `yes` (need repo access, design files, API docs)
- Decision-maker proximity: `direct` (founder/CTO, not procurement)
