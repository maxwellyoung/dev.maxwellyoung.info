# Offer Sheet

Define what you sell so you stop underpricing.

## Offer 1: Design Engineering Sprint (primary)

- **Name**: Design Engineering Sprint
- **Format**: 4-8 week engagement, embedded in your team
- **Deliverable**: Production-ready mobile or web app with polished interactions
- **Stack**: React Native (Expo), Next.js, TypeScript, motion design
- **Price floor**: $3,000 USD/mo (retainer) or $15,000 USD (fixed project)
- **Ideal client**: Seed/Series A startup, 5-20 people, shipping a consumer product
- **What's included**: Code, design implementation, App Store submission pipeline, weekly check-ins
- **What's NOT included**: Backend infra, brand design, ongoing maintenance (separate retainer)

## Offer 2: App Store Ship (secondary)

- **Name**: App Store Ship
- **Format**: 2-4 week fixed engagement
- **Deliverable**: Take an existing React Native / Expo app from dev to App Store
- **Price floor**: $5,000 USD (fixed)
- **Ideal client**: Indie founder or small team with a working app that needs production polish + submission
- **What's included**: Build pipeline setup, TestFlight, App Store submission, first review cycle
- **What's NOT included**: Feature development, backend, design

## Offer 3: Music + Creative Tech (exploratory)

- **Name**: Creative Tech Build
- **Format**: Project-based
- **Deliverable**: Custom interactive experience (spatial canvas, generative visuals, music tools)
- **Price floor**: $8,000 USD (project)
- **Ideal client**: Labels, artists, creative studios wanting something unique
- **What's included**: Concept, build, deploy
- **Proof**: Liner, jeremy-blake generative art, 1kiss music video tooling
